mujh se ankhen pher ke tu ne ye mushkil bhi asan kar di  
varna tere gham ke badle leta kaun basere dil men

kanton ke paivand laga kar tu ne phuul bikhere dil men

chain ke badle dard ne ab to daal diye hain dere dil men  
darte darte soch raha huun vo mere hain ab bhi shayad  
varna kaun kiya karta hai yuun pheron par phere dil men  
pyaar ki pahli manzil par anjan musafir dekh raha hai  
ankhon men sangin ujale aur sayyal andhere dil men  
ujdi yaado tuute sapno shayad kuchh malum ho tum ko  
kaun uthata hai rah rah kar tisen sham-savere dil men
