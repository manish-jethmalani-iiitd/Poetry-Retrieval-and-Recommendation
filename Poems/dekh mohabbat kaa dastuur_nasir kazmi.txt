tu mujh se main tujh se duur  
tanha tanha phirte hain  
dil viran ankhen be-nur  
dost bichhadte jaate hain  
shauq liye jaata hai duur  
ham apna gham bhuul ga.e  
aaj kise dekha majbur  
dil ki dhadkan kahti hai  
aaj koi aa.ega zarur  
koshish lazim hai pyare  
aage jo us ko manzur  
suraj duub chala 'nasir'  
aur abhi manzil hai duur
