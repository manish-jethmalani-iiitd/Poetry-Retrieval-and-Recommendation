ranj-e-firaq-e-yar men rusva nahin hua  
itna main chup hua ki tamasha nahin hua  
aisa safar hai jis men koi ham-safar nahin  
rasta hai is tarah ka jo dekha nahin hua  
mushkil hua hai rahna hamen is dayar men  
barson yahan rahe hain ye apna nahin hua  
vo kaam shah-e-shahr se ya shahr se hua  
jo kaam bhi hua hai vo achchha nahin hua  
milna tha ek baar use phir kahin 'munir'  
aisa men chahta tha par aisa nahin hua
