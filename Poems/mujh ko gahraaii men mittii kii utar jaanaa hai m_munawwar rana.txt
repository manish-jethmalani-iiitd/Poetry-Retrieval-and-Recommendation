mujh ko gahra.i men mitti ki utar jaana hai  
zindagi bandh le saman-e-safar jaana hai  
ghar ki dahliz pe raushan hain vo bujhti ankhen  
mujh ko mat rok mujhe laut ke ghar jaana hai  
main vo mele men bhatakta hua ik bachcha huun  
jis ke maan baap ko rote hue mar jaana hai  
zindagi taash ke patton ki tarah hai meri  
aur patton ko bahar-hal bikhar jaana hai  
ek be-nam se rishte ki tamanna le kar  
is kabutar ko kisi chhat pe utar jaana hai
