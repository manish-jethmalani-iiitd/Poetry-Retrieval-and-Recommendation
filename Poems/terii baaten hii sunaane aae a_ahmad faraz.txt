teri baten hi sunane aa.e  
dost bhi dil hi dukhane aa.e  
phuul khilte hain to ham sochte hain  
tere aane ke zamane aa.e  
aisi kuchh chup si lagi hai jaise  
ham tujhe haal sunane aa.e  
ishq tanha hai sar-e-manzil-e-gham  
kaun ye bojh uthane aa.e  
ajnabi dost hamen dekh ki ham  
kuchh tujhe yaad dilane aa.e  
dil dhadakta hai safar ke hangam  
kaash phir koi bulane aa.e  
ab to rone se bhi dil dukhta hai  
shayad ab hosh thikane aa.e  
kya kahin phir koi basti ujdi  
log kyuun jashn manane aa.e  
so raho maut ke pahlu men 'faraz'  
niind kis vaqt na jaane aa.e
