mashuq-e-shokh o ashiq-e-divana chahiye  
us lab se mil hi ja.ega bosa kabhi to haan  
shauq-e-fuzul o jur.at-e-rindana chahiye  
ashiq naqab-e-jalva-e-janana chahiye  
fanus-e-sham.a ko par-e-parvana chahiye

mashuq-e-shokh o aashiq-e-diwana chahiye  
us lab se mil hi jaega bosa kabhi to han  
shauq-e-fuzul o jurat-e-rindana chahiye  
aashiq naqab-e-jalwa-e-jaanana chahiye  
fanus-e-shama ko par-e-parwana chahiye
