ab khushi hai na koi dard rulane vaala  
ham ne apna liya har rang zamane vaala  
ek be-chehra si ummid hai chehra chehra  
jis taraf dekhiye aane ko hai aane vaala  
us ko rukhsat to kiya tha mujhe ma.alum na tha  
saara ghar le gaya ghar chhod ke jaane vaala  
duur ke chand ko dhundo na kisi anchal men  
ye ujala nahin angan men samane vaala  
ik musafir ke safar jaisi hai sab ki duniya  
koi jaldi men koi der se jaane vaala  
ab khushi hai na koi dard rulane wala  
hum ne apna liya har rang zamane wala  
ek be-chehra si ummid hai chehra chehra  
jis taraf dekhiye aane ko hai aane wala  
us ko rukhsat to kiya tha mujhe malum na tha  
sara ghar le gaya ghar chhod ke jaane wala  
dur ke chand ko dhundo na kisi aanchal mein  
ye ujala nahin aangan mein samane wala  
ek musafir ke safar jaisi hai sab ki duniya  
koi jaldi mein koi der se jaane wala
