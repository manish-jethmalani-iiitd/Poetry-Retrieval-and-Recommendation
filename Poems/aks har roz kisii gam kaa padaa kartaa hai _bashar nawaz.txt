dil vo a.ina ki chup-chap taka karta hai  
bahte paani ki tarah dard ki bhi shakl nahin  
jab bhi milta hai naya ruup hua karta hai  
main to bahrup huun us ka jo hai mere andar  
vo koi aur hai jo mujh men jiya karta hai  
rang sa roz bikhar jaata hai divaron par  
kuchh diye jaisa dariche men jala karta hai  
jaane vo kaun hai jo raat ke sannate men  
kabhi rota hai kabhi khud pe hinsa karta hai  
roz rahon se guzarta hai sadaon ka julus  
dil ka sannata magar roz badha karta hai
