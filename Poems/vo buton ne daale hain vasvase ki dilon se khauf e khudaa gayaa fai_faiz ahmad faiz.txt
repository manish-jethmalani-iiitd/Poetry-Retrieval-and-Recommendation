vo padi hain roz qayamaten ki khayal-e-roz-e-jaza gaya  
jo nafas tha khar-e-gulu bana jo uthe the haath lahu hue  
vo nashat-e-ah-e-sahr ga.i vo vaqar-e-dast-e-dua gaya  
na vo rang fasl-e-bahar ka na ravish vo abr-e-bahar ki  
jis ada se yaar the ashna vo mizaj-e-bad-e-saba gaya  
jo talab pe ahd-e-vafa kiya to vo abru-e-vafa ga.i  
sar-e-am jab hue mudda.i to savab-e-sidq-o-safa gaya  
abhi badban ko tah rakho abhi muztarib hai rukh-e-hava  
kisi raste men hai muntazir vo sukun jo aa ke chala gaya
