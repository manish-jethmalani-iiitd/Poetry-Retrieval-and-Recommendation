na takht-o-taj men ne lashkar-o-sipah men hai  
jo baat mard-e-qalandar ki bargah men hai  
sanam-kada hai jahan aur mard-e-haq hai khalil  
ye nukta vo hai ki poshida la-ilah men hai  
vahi jahan hai tira jis ko tu kare paida  
ye sang-o-khisht nahin jo tiri nigah men hai  
mah o sitara se aage maqam hai jis ka  
vo musht-e-khak abhi avargan-e-rah men hai  
khabar mili hai khudayan-e-bahr-o-bar se mujhe  
farang rahguzar-e-sail-e-be-panah men hai  
talash us ki fazaon men kar nasib apna  
jahan-e-taza miri ah-e-sub.h-gah men hai  
mire kadu ko ghanimat samajh ki bada-e-nab  
na madrase men hai baaqi na khanqah men hai

ye nukta wo hai ki poshida la-ilah mein hai  
wahi jahan hai tera jis ko tu kare paida  
ye sang-o-khisht nahin jo teri nigah mein hai

wo musht-e-khak abhi aawargan-e-rah mein hai
