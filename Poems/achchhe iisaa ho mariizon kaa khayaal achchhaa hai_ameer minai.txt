ham mare jaate hain tum kahte ho haal achchha hai  
tujh se mangun main tujhi ko ki sabhi kuchh mil jaa.e  
sau savalon se yahi ek saval achchha hai  
dekh le bulbul o parvana ki betabi ko  
hijr achchha na hasinon ka visal achchha hai  
aa gaya us ka tasavvur to pukara ye shauq  
dil men jam jaa.e ilahi ye khayal achchha hai  
ankhen dikhlate ho joban to dikhao sahab

barq agar garmi-e-raftar men achchhi hai 'amir'  
garmi-e-husn men vo barq-jamal achchha hai
