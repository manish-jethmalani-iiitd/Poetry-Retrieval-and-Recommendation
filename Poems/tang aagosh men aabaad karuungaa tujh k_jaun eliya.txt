tang aghosh men abad karunga tujh ko  
huun bahut shaad ki nashad karunga tujh ko  
fikr-e-ijad men gum huun mujhe ghafil na samajh  
apne andaz par ijad karunga tujh ko  
nashsha hai raah ki duuri ka ki hamrah hai tu  
jaane kis shahr men abad karunga tujh ko  
meri banhon men bahakne ki saza bhi sun le  
ab bahut der men azad karunga tujh ko  
main ki rahta huun ba-sad-naz gurezan tujh se  
tu na hoga to bahut yaad karunga tujh ko
