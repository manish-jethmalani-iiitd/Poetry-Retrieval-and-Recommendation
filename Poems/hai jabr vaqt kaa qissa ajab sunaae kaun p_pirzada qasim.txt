hai jabr vaqt ka qissa ajab suna.e kaun  
main yaad us ko karun aur yaad aa.e kaun  
ye baat bujhte diyon ne kisi se puchhi thi  
jale to ham the magar khair jagmaga.e kaun  
use talash to karna hai phir ye sochta huun  
zamana aur hai ab zahmaten utha.e kaun  
yahan to apne charaghon ki fikr hai sab ko  
diya jalaya hai sab ne diye jala.e kaun  
yahan to log inhi hairaton men jiite hain  
ki tiir kis pe chale aur zakhm khaa.e kaun  
yahan to jagti ankhon men khvab jagte hain  
jo jagte hon unhen khvab se jaga.e kaun  
yahan kisi ko kisi ki khabar nahin milti  
magar ye be-khabari ki khabar suna.e kaun  
yahan to sub.h se pahle hi bazm barham hai  
diya bujha de koi par diya bujha.e kaun
