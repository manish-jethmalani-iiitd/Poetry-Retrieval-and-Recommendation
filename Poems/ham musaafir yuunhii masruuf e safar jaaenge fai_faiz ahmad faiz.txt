ham musafir yunhi masruf-e-safar ja.enge  
be-nishan ho ga.e jab shahr to ghar ja.enge  
kis qadar hoga yahan mehr-o-vafa ka matam  
ham tiri yaad se jis roz utar ja.enge  
jauhari band kiye jaate hain bazar-e-sukhan  
ham kise bechne almas-o-guhar ja.enge  
nemat-e-zist ka ye qarz chukega kaise  
laakh ghabra ke ye kahte rahen mar ja.enge  
shayad apna bhi koi bait hudi-khvan ban kar  
saath ja.ega mire yaar jidhar ja.enge  
'faiz' aate hain rah-e-ishq men jo sakht maqam  
aane valon se kaho ham to guzar ja.enge  
hum musafir yunhi masruf-e-safar jaenge  
be-nishan ho gae jab shahr to ghar jaenge  
kis qadar hoga yahan mehr-o-wafa ka matam  
hum teri yaad se jis roz utar jaenge  
jauhari band kiye jate hain bazar-e-sukhan  
hum kise bechne almas-o-guhar jaenge  
nemat-e-zist ka ye qarz chukega kaise  
lakh ghabra ke ye kahte rahen mar jaenge  
shayad apna bhi koi bait hudi-khwan ban kar  
sath jaega mere yar jidhar jaenge  
'faiz' aate hain rah-e-ishq mein jo sakht maqam  
aane walon se kaho hum to guzar jaenge
