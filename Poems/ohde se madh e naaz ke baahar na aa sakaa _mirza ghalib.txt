ohde se mad.h-e-naz ke bahar na aa saka  
gar ik ada ho to use apni qaza kahun  
halqe hain chashm-ha-e-kushada ba-su-e-dil  
har tar-e-zulf ko nigah-e-surma-sa kahun  
main aur sad-hazar nava-e-jigar-kharash  
tu aur ek vo na-shunidan ki kya kahun  
zalim mire guman se mujhe munfa.il na chaah  
hai hai khuda-na-karda tujhe bevafa kahun
