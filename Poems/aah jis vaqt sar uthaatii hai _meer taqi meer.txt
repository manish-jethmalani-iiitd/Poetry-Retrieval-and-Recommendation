arsh par barchhiyan chalati hai  
naz-bardaar-e-lab hai jaan jab se  
tere khat ki khabar ko paati hai  
ai shab-e-hijr raast kah tujh ko  
baat kuchh sub.h ki bhi aati hai  
chashm-e-bad-dur-chasm-e-tar ai 'mir'  
ankhen tufan ko dikhati hai
