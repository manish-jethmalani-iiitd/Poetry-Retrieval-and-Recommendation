har ek baat ko chup-chap kyuun suna jaa.e  
kabhi to hausla kar ke nahin kaha jaa.e  
tumhara ghar bhi isi shahr ke hisar men hai  
lagi hai aag kahan kyuun pata kiya jaa.e  
juda hai hiir se ranjha ka.i zamanon se  
na.e sire se kahani ko phir likha jaa.e  
kaha gaya hai sitaron ko chhuna mushkil hai  
ye kitna sach hai kabhi tajraba kiya jaa.e  
kitaben yuun to bahut si hain mere baare men  
kabhi akele men khud ko bhi padh liya jaa.e

kabhi to hausla kar ke nahin kaha jae

lagi hai aag kahan kyun pata kiya jae

nae sire se kahani ko phir likha jae  
kaha gaya hai sitaron ko chhuna mushkil hai  
ye kitna sach hai kabhi tajraba kiya jae  
kitaben yun to bahut si hain mere bare mein
