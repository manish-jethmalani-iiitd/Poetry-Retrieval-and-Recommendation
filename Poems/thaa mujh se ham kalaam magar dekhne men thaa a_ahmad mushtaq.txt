jaane vo kis khayal men tha kis samay men tha  
kaise makan ujaad hua kis se puchhte  
chulhe men raushni thi na paani ghade men tha  
ta-sub.h barg-o-shakh-o-shajar jhumte rahe  
kal shab bala ka soz hava ke gale men tha  
nindon men phir raha huun use dhundhta hua  
shamil jo ek khvab mire rat-jage men tha
