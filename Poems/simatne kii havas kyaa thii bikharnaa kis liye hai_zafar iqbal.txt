simatne ki havas kya thi bikharna kis liye hai  
vo jiina kis ki khatir tha ye marna kis liye hai  
mohabbat bhi hai aur apna taqaza bhi nahin kuchh  
ham us se saaf kah denge mukarna kis liye hai  
jhijhakna hai to us ke samne hona hi kaisa  
jo darna hai to dariya men utarna kis liye hai  
masafat khvab hai to khvab men ab jagna kya  
agar chal hi pade hain to thaharna kis liye hai  
na karne se bhi hota ho jahan sab ka guzara  
vahan akhir kisi ne kaam karna kis liye hai  
agar rukna nahin us ne hamare paas to phir  
hamare raste par se guzarna kis liye hai  
vo kah dega to uth ja.enge us ki bazm se ham  
munasib hi nahin lagta pasarna kis liye hai  
'zafar' us par asar to koi hota hai na hoga  
to phir ye roz ka banna sanvarna kis liye hai
