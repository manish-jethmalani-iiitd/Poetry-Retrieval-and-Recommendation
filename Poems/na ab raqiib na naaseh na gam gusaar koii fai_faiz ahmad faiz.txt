na ab raqib na naseh na gham-gusar koi  
tum ashna the to thiin ashna.iyan kya kya  
juda the ham to mayassar thiin qurbaten kitni  
baham hue to padi hain juda.iyan kya kya  
pahunch ke dar pe tire kitne mo'tabar thahre  
agarche rah men huiin jag-hansa.iyan kya kya  
ham aise sada-dilon ki niyaz-mandi se  
buton ne ki hain jahan men khuda.iyan kya kya  
sitam pe khush kabhi lutf-o-karam se ranjida  
sikha.in tum ne hamen kaj-ada.iyan kya kya  
na ab raqib na naseh na gham-gusar koi  
tum aashna the to thin aashnaiyan kya kya  
juda the hum to mayassar thin qurbaten kitni  
baham hue to padi hain judaiyan kya kya  
pahunch ke dar pe tere kitne mo'tabar thahre  
agarche rah mein huin jag-hansaiyan kya kya  
hum aise sada-dilon ki niyaz-mandi se  
buton ne ki hain jahan mein khudaiyan kya kya  
sitam pe khush kabhi lutf-o-karam se ranjida  
sikhain tum ne hamein kaj-adaiyan kya kya
