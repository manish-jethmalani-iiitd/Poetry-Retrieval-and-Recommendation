sarapa rehn-e-ishq-o-na-guzir-e-ulfat-e-hasti  
ibadat barq ki karta huun aur afsos hasil ka  
ba-qadr-e-zarf hai saaqi khumar-e-tishna-kami bhi  
jo tu dariya-e-mai hai to main khamyaza huun sahil ka  
zi-bas khun-gashta-e-rashk-e-vafa tha vahm bismil ka  
churaya zakhm-ha-e-dil ne paani tegh-e-qatil ka  
nigah-e-chashm-e-hasid vaam le ai zauq-e-khud-bini  
tamasha.i huun vahdat-khana-e-a.ina-e-dil ka  
sharar-fursat nigah saman-e-yak-alam charaghan hai  
ba-qadr-e-rang yaan gardish men hai paimana mahfil ka  
sarasar takhtan ko shash-jihat yak-arsa jaulan tha  
hua vamandgi se rah-ravan ki farq manzil ka  
mujhe rah-e-sukhan men khauf-e-gum-rahi nahin ghalib  
asa-e-khizr-e-sahra-e-sukhan hai khama be-dil ka
