fitrat ko khirad ke ru-ba-ru kar  
taskhir-e-maqam-e-rang-o-bu kar  
tu apni khudi ko kho chuka hai  
khoi hui shai ki justuju kar  
taron ki faza hai be-karana  
tu bhi ye maqam-e-arzu kar  
uryan hain tire chaman ki huren  
chak-e-gul-o-lala ko rafu kar  
be-zauq nahin agarche fitrat  
jo us se na ho saka vo tu kar
