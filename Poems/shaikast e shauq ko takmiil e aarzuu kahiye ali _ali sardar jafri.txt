shaikast-e-shauq ko takmil-e-arzu kahiye  
jo tishnagi ho to paimana-o-subu kahiye  
khayal-e-yar ko dijiye visal-e-yar ka naam  
shab-e-firaq ko gaisu-e-mushk-bu kahiye  
charagh-e-anjuman-e-hairat-e-nazara the  
vo lala-ru jinhen ab dagh-e-arzu kahiye  
mahak rahi hai ghazal zikr-e-zulf-e-khuban se  
nasim-e-sub.h ki manind ku-ba-ku kahiye  
shikayaten bhi bahut hain hikayaten bhi bahut  
maza to jab hai ki yaron ke ru-ba-ru kahiye  
ba-hukm kijiye phir khanjaron ki dildari  
dahan-e-zakhm se afsana-e-gulu kahiye
