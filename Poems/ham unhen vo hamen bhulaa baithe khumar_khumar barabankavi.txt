andhiyo jaao ab karo aram

ro ke ham lutf-e-gham ganva baithe

uth ke ik bevafa ne de di jaan  
rah ga.e saare ba-vafa baithe  
hashr ka din abhi hai duur 'khumar'  
aap kyuun zahidon men ja baithe
