hamin se apni nava ham-kalam hoti rahi  
ye tegh apne lahu men niyam hoti rahi  
muqabil-e-saf-e-a.ada jise kiya aghaz  
vo jang apne hi dil men tamam hoti rahi  
koi masiha na ifa-e-ahd ko pahuncha  
bahut talash pas-e-qatl-e-am hoti rahi  
ye barhaman ka karam vo ata-e-shaikh-e-haram  
kabhi hayat kabhi mai haram hoti rahi  
jo kuchh bhi ban na pada 'faiz' lut ke yaron se  
to rahzanon se dua-o-salam hoti rahi
