ummid se kam chashm-e-kharidar men aa.e  
ham log zara der se bazar men aa.e  
sach khud se bhi ye log nahin bolne vaale  
ai ahl-e-junun tum yahan bekar men aa.e  
ye aag havas ki hai jhulas degi ise bhi  
suraj se kaho saya-e-divar men aa.e  
badhti hi chali jaati hai tanha.i hamari  
kya soch ke ham vadi-e-inkar men aa.e  
ummid se kam chashm-e-kharidar mein aae  
hum log zara der se bazar mein aae  
sach khud se bhi ye log nahin bolne wale  
ai ahl-e-junun tum yahan bekar mein aae  
ye aag hawas ki hai jhulas degi ise bhi  
suraj se kaho saya-e-diwar mein aae  
badhti hi chali jati hai tanhai hamari  
kya soch ke hum wadi-e-inkar mein aae
