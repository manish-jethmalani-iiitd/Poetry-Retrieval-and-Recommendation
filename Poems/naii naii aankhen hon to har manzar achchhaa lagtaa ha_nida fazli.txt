kuchh din shahr men ghume lekin ab ghar achchha lagta hai  
milne-julne valon men to sab hi apne jaise hain  
jis se ab tak mile nahin vo akasr achchha lagta hai  
mere angan men aa.e ya tere sar par chot lage  
sannaton men bolne vaala patthar achchha lagta hai  
chahat ho ya puuja sab ke apne apne sanche hain  
jo maut men dhal jaa.e vo paikar achchha lagta hai  
ham ne bhi so kar dekha hai na.e purane shahron men  
jaisa bhi hai apne ghar ka bistar achchha lagta hai
