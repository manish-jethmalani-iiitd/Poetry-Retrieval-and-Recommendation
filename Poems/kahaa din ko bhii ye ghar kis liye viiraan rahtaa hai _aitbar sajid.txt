yahan kya ham sa koi be-sar-o-saman rahta hai  
dar-o-divar sannate ki chadar men hain khvabida  
bhala aisi jagah zinda koi insan rahta hai  
musalsal puchhne par ek chilman se javab aaya  
yahan ik dil-shikasta sahab-e-divan rahta hai  
jhijak kar main ne puchha kya kabhi bahar nahin aata  
javab aaya use khalvat men itminan rahta hai  
kaha kya us ke rishta-dar bhi milne nahin aate  
javab aaya ye sahra rat-din sunsan rahta hai  
kaha koi to hoga us ke dukh-sukh bantne vaala  
javab aaya nahin khali ye ghar ye lawn rahta hai

javab aaya khayal us ka mujhe har aan rahta hai  
khuda ka shukr hai ham ik faza men saans lete hain  
agar milte nahin itna to itminan rahta hai
