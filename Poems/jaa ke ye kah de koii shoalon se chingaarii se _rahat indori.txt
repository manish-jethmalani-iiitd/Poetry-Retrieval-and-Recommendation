log asan hue hain badi dushvari se  
zehn men jab bhi tire khat ki ibarat chamki

chaliye mil aate hain chal kar kisi darbari se  
badshahon se bhi phenke hue sikke na liye  
ham ne khairat bhi mangi hai to khuddari se
