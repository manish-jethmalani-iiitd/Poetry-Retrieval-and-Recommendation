jo samjhate bhi aa kar va.iz-e-barham to kya karte  
ham is duniya ke aage us jahan ka gham to kya karte  
haram se mai-kade tak manzil-e-yak-umr thi saaqi  
sahara gar na deti laghzish-e-paiham to kya karte  
jo mitti ko mizaj-e-gul ata kar den vo ai vaa.iz  
zamin se duur fikr-e-jannat-e-adam to kya karte  
saval un ka javab un ka sukut un ka khitab un ka  
ham un ki anjuman men sar na karte kham to kya karte  
jahan 'majruh' dil ke hausle tuten nigahon se  
vahan karte bhi marg-e-shauq ka matam to kya karte

haram se mai-kade tak manzil-e-yak-umr thi saqi  
sahaara gar na deti laghzish-e-paiham to kya karte  
jo mitti ko mizaj-e-gul ata kar den wo ai waiz  
zamin se dur fikr-e-jannat-e-adam to kya karte  
sawal un ka jawab un ka sukut un ka khitab un ka  
hum un ki anjuman mein sar na karte kham to kya karte  
jahan 'majruh' dil ke hausle tuten nigahon se  
wahan karte bhi marg-e-shauq ka matam to kya karte
