ajnabi hairan mat hona ki dar khulta nahin  
jo yahan abad hain un par bhi ghar khulta nahin  
raste kab gard ho jaate hain aur manzil sarab  
har musafir par tilism-e-rahguzar khulta nahin  
dekhne vaale taghaful kar-farma hai abhi  
vo daricha khul gaya husn-e-nazar khulta nahin  
jaane kyuun teri taraf se dil ko dhadka hi raha  
is takalluf se to koi nama-bar khulta nahin  
intizar aur dastakon ke darmiyan katti hai umr  
itni asani se to bab-e-hunar khulta nahin  
ham bhi us ke saath gardish men hain barson se 'salim'  
jo sitara saath rahta hai magar khulta nahin
