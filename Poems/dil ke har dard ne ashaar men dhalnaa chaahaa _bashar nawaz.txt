apna pairahan-e-be-rang badalna chaha  
koi an-jani si taqat thi jo aade aa.i  
varna ham ne to bahar-gam sambhalna chaha  
chahte to kisi patthar ki tarah ji lete  
ham ne khud mom ki manind pighalna chaha  
ankhen jalne lagin tapte hue bazaron men  
dil ne jab bhi kisi manzar pe machalna chaha  
sirf ham hi nahin har ek ne jiine ke liye  
ik na ik jhute sahare se bahalna chaha  
kaun hai ye jo sisakta hai mire siine men
