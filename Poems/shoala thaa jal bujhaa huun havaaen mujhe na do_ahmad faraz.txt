sho.ala tha jal-bujha huun hava.en mujhe na do  
main kab ka ja chuka huun sada.en mujhe na do  
jo zahr pi chuka huun tumhin ne mujhe diya  
ab tum to zindagi ki dua.en mujhe na do  
ye bhi bada karam hai salamat hai jism abhi  
ai khusravan-e-shahr qaba.en mujhe na do  
aisa na ho kabhi ki palat kar na aa sakun  
har baar duur ja ke sada.en mujhe na do  
kab mujh ko e'tiraf-e-mohabbat na tha 'faraz'  
kab main ne ye kaha hai saza.en mujhe na do

ai khusrawan-e-shahr qabaen mujhe na do
