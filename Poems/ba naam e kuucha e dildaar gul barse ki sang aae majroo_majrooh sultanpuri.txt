ba-nam-e-kucha-e-dildar gul barse ki sang aa.e  
hansa hai chak-e-pairahan na kyuun chehre pe rang aa.e  
bachate phirte akhir kab talak dast-e-azizan se  
unhin ko saunp kar ham to kulah-e-nam-o-nang aa.e  
hanso mat ahl-e-dil apni si jaano bazm-e-khuban men  
chale aa.e idhar ham bhi bahut jab dil se tang aa.e  
kahan sehn-e-chaman men baat ku-e-sar-faroshan ki  
idhar se sada-ru nikle udhar se lala-rang aa.e  
karo 'majruh' tab dar-o-rasan ke tazkire ham se  
jab us qamat ke saa.e men tumhen jiine ka dhang aa.e

hansa hai chaak-e-pairahan na kyun chehre pe rang aae  
bachate phirte aakhir kab talak dast-e-azizan se  
unhin ko saunp kar hum to kulah-e-nam-o-nang aae  
hanso mat ahl-e-dil apni si jaano bazm-e-khuban mein  
chale aae idhar hum bhi bahut jab dil se tang aae  
kahan sehn-e-chaman mein baat ku-e-sar-faroshan ki  
idhar se sada-ru nikle udhar se lala-rang aae  
karo 'majruh' tab dar-o-rasan ke tazkire hum se  
jab us qamat ke sae mein tumhein jine ka dhang aae
