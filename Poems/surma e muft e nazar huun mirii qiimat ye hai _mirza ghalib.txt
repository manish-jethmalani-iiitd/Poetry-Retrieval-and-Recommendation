surma-e-muft-e-nazar huun miri qimat ye hai  
ki rahe chashm-e-kharidar pe ehsan mera  
rukhsat-e-nala mujhe de ki mabada zalim  
tere chehre se ho zahir gham-e-pinhan mera  
khalvat-e-abila-e-pa men hai jaulan mera  
khuun hai dil tangi-e-vahshat se bayaban mera  
hasrat-e-nashsha-e-vahshat na ba-sa.i-e-dil hai  
arz-e-khamiyaza-e-majnun hai gareban mera  
fahm zanjiri-e-be-rabti-e-dil hai ya-rab  
kis zaban men hai laqab khvab-e-pareshan mera
