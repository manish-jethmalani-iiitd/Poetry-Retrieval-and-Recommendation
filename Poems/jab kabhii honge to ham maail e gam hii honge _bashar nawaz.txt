jab kabhi honge to ham ma.il-e-gham hi honge  
aise divane bhi is daur men kam hi honge  
ham to zakhmon pe bhi ye soch ke khush hote hain  
tohfa-e-dost hain jab ye to karam hi honge  
bazm-e-alam men jab aa.e hain to baithen kuchh aur  
bas yahi hoga na kuchh aur sitam hi honge  
jab bhi barbad-e-vafa koi nazar aa.e tumhen  
ghaur se dekh liya karna vo ham hi honge  
koi bhatka hua badal koi udti khushbu  
kaun kah sakta hai ik din ye baham hi honge  
jab kabhi honge to hum mail-e-gham hi honge  
aise diwane bhi is daur mein kam hi honge  
hum to zakhmon pe bhi ye soch ke khush hote hain  
tohfa-e-dost hain jab ye to karam hi honge  
bazm-e-alam mein jab aae hain to baithen kuchh aur  
bas yahi hoga na kuchh aur sitam hi honge  
jab bhi barbaad-e-wafa koi nazar aae tumhein  
ghaur se dekh liya karna wo hum hi honge  
koi bhatka hua baadal koi udti khushbu  
kaun kah sakta hai ek din ye baham hi honge
