uljhe kanton se ki khele gul-e-tar se pahle  
fikr ye hai ki saba aa.e kidhar se pahle  
jam-o-paimana-o-saqi ka guman tha lekin  
dida-e-tar hi tha yaan dida-e-tar se pahle  
abr-e-naisan ki na barakat hai na faizan-e-bahar  
qatre gum ho ga.e ta.amir-e-guhar se pahle  
jam gaya dil men lahu suukh ga.e ankhon men ashk  
tham gaya dard-e-jigar rang-e-sahar se pahle  
qafile aa.e to the na.aron ke parcham le kar  
sar-nigun ho ga.i har aah asar se pahle  
khun-e-sar bah gaya maut aa ga.i divanon ko  
barish-e-sang se tufan-e-sharar se pahle  
surkhi-e-khun-e-tamanna ki mahak aati hai  
dil koi tuuta hai shayad gul-e-tar se pahle  
maqtal-e-shauq ke adab nirale hain bahut  
dil bhi qatil ko diya karte hain sar se pahle
