vaqt ka kya hai guzarta hai guzar ja.ega  
itna manus na ho khalvat-e-gham se apni  
tu kabhi khud ko bhi dekhega to dar ja.ega  
dubte dubte kashti ko uchhala de duun  
main nahin koi to sahil pe utar ja.ega  
zindagi teri ata hai to ye jaane vaala  
teri bakhshish tiri dahliz pe dhar ja.ega  
zabt lazim hai magar dukh hai qayamat ka 'faraz'  
zalim ab ke bhi na ro.ega to mar ja.ega
