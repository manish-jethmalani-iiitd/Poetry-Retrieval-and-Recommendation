jab raat ga.e tiri yaad aa.i sau tarah se ji ko bahlaya  
kabhi apne hi dil se baten kiin kabhi teri yaad ko samjhaya  
yunhi vaqt ganvaya moti sa yunhi umr ganva.i sona si  
sach kahte ho tum ham-sukhano is ishq men ham ne kya paaya  
jab pahle-pahal tujhe dekha tha dil kitne zor se dhadka tha  
vo lahr na phir dil men jaagi vo vaqt na laut ke phir aaya  
phir aaj tire darvaze par badi der ke ba.ad gaya tha magar  
ik baat achanak yaad aa.i main bahar hi se laut aaya  
jab raat gae teri yaad aai sau tarah se ji ko bahlaya  
kabhi apne hi dil se baaten kin kabhi teri yaad ko samjhaya  
yunhi waqt ganwaya moti sa yunhi umr ganwai sona si  
sach kahte ho tum ham-sukhano is ishq mein hum ne kya paya  
jab pahle-pahal tujhe dekha tha dil kitne zor se dhadka tha  
wo lahr na phir dil mein jagi wo waqt na laut ke phir aaya  
phir aaj tere darwaze par badi der ke baad gaya tha magar  
ek baat achanak yaad aai main bahar hi se laut aaya
