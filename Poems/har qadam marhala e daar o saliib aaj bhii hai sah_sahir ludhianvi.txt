jo kabhi tha vahi insan ka nasib aaj bhi hai  
jagmagate hain ufuq par ye sitare lekin  
rasta manzil-e-hasti ka muhib aaj bhi hai  
sar-e-maqtal jinhen jaana tha vo ja bhi pahunche  
sar-e-mimbar koi mohtat khatib aaj bhi hai  
ahl-e-danish ne jise amr-e-musallam maana  
ahl-e-dil ke liye vo baat ajiib aaj bhi hai  
ye tiri yaad hai ya meri aziyat-koshi  
ek nashtar sa rag-e-jan ke qarib aaj bhi hai  
kaun jaane ye tira sha.er-e-ashufta-e-mizaj  
kitne maghrur khudaon ka raqib aaj bhi hai
