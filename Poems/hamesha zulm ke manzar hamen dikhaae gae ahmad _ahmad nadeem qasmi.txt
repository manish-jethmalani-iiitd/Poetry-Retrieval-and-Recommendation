pahad tode ga.e aur mahal bana.e ga.e  
tulu-e-sub.h ki afvah itni aam hui  
ki nisf shab ko gharon ke diye bujha.e ga.e  
ab ek baar to qudrat javab-dah thahre  
hazar baar ham insan azma.e ga.e  
falak ka tantana bhi tuut kar zamin pe gira

to phir kalim sar-e-tur kyuun bula.e ga.e
