kya din vo mubarak the kya khuub thiin vo raten  
auron se chhute dilbar dil-dar hove mera  
bar-haq hain agar pairav kuchh tum men karamaten  
kal lad ga.iin kuche men ankhon se miri ankhiyan  
kuchh zor hi aapas men do do huiin samghaten  
kashmir si jagah men na-shukr na rah zahid  
jannat men tu ai giidi maare hai ye kyuun laten  
is ishq ke kuche men zahid tu sambhal chalna  
kuchh pesh na javengi yaan teri munajaten  
us roz miyan mil kar nazron ko churate the
