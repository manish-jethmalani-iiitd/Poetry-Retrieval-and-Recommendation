justuju jis ki thi us ko to na paaya ham ne  
is bahane se magar dekh li duniya ham ne  
sab ka ahval vahi hai jo hamara hai aaj  
ye alag baat ki shikva kiya tanha ham ne  
khud pashiman hue ne use sharminda kiya  
ishq ki vaz.a ko kya khuub nibhaya ham ne  
kaun sa qahr ye ankhon pe hua hai nazil  
ek muddat se koi khvab na dekha ham ne  
umr bhar sach hi kaha sach ke siva kuchh na kaha  
ajr kya is ka milega ye na socha ham ne
