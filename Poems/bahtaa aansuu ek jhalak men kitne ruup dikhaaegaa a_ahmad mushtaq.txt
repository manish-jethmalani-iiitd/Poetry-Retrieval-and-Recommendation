bahta aansu ek jhalak men kitne ruup dikha.ega  
aankh se ho kar gaal bhigo kar mitti men mil ja.ega  
bhulne vale! vaqt ke aivanon men kaun thaharta hai  
biiti shaam ke darvaze par kis ko bulane aa.ega  
ankh-micholi khel raha hai ik badli se ik taara  
phir badli ki yurish hogi phir taara chhup ja.ega  
andhiyare ke ghor-nagar men ek kiran abad hui  
kis ko khabar hai pahla jhonka kitne phuul khila.ega  
phir ik lamha aan ruka hai vaqt ke suune sahra men  
pal bhar apni chhab dikhla kar lamhon men mil ja.ega  
bahta aansu ek jhalak mein kitne rup dikhaega  
aankh se ho kar gal bhigo kar mitti mein mil jaega  
bhulne wale! waqt ke aiwanon mein kaun thaharta hai  
biti sham ke darwaze par kis ko bulane aaega  
aankh-micholi khel raha hai ek badli se ek tara  
phir badli ki yurish hogi phir tara chhup jaega  
andhiyare ke ghor-nagar mein ek kiran aabaad hui  
kis ko khabar hai pahla jhonka kitne phul khilaega  
phir ek lamha aan ruka hai waqt ke sune sahra mein  
pal bhar apni chhab dikhla kar lamhon mein mil jaega
