dil ki aag javani ke rukhsaron ko dahka.e hai  
bahe pasina mukhde par ya suraj pighla jaa.e hai  
man ik nanha sa balak hai humak humak rah jaa.e hai  
duur se mukh ka chand dikha kar kaun use lalcha.e hai  
mai hai teri ankhon men aur mujh pe nasha sa taari hai  
niind hai teri palkon men aur khvab mujhe dikhla.e hai  
tere qamat ki larzish se mauj-e-mai men larzish hai  
teri nigah ki masti hi paimanon ko chhalka.e hai  
tera dard salamat hai to marne ki ummid nahin  
laakh dukhi ho ye duniya rahne ki jagah ban jaa.e hai  
dil ki aag jawani ke rukhsaron ko dahkae hai  
bahe pasina mukhde par ya suraj pighla jae hai  
man ek nanha sa baalak hai humak humak rah jae hai  
dur se mukh ka chand dikha kar kaun use lalchae hai  
mai hai teri aankhon mein aur mujh pe nasha sa tari hai  
nind hai teri palkon mein aur khwab mujhe dikhlae hai  
tere qamat ki larzish se mauj-e-mai mein larzish hai  
teri nigah ki masti hi paimanon ko chhalkae hai  
tera dard salamat hai to marne ki ummid nahin  
lakh dukhi ho ye duniya rahne ki jagah ban jae hai
