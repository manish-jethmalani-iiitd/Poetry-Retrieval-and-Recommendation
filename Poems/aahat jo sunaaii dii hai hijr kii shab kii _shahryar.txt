ye raa.e akeli meri nahin hai sab ki hai  
sunsan sadak sannate aur lambe saa.e  
ye saari faza ai dil tere matlab ki hai  
tiri diid se ankhen ji bhar ke sairab huiin  
kis roz hua tha aisa baat ye kab ki hai  
tujhe bhuul gaya kabhi yaad nahin karta tujh ko  
jo baat bahut pahle karni thi ab ki hai  
mire suraj aa! mire jism pe apna saaya kar  
badi tez hava hai sardi aaj ghazab ki hai
