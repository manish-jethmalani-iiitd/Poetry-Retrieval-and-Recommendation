ai diida khanuman tu hamara dubo saka  
lekin ghubar yaar ke dil se na dho saka  
tujh husn ne diya na kabhu mufsidi ko chain  
fitna na tere daur men phir niind so saka  
jo sham.a-tan hua shab-e-hijran men sarf-e-ashk  
par jis qadar main chahe tha utna na ro saka  
'sauda' qimar-e-ishq men shirin se kohkan  
baazi agarche pa na saka sar to kho saka  
kis munh se phir tu aap ko kahta hai ishq-baz  
ai ru-siyah tujh se to ye bhi na ho saka
