tum se nahin milne ka irada to hai lekin  
tum se na milenge ye qasam bhi nahin khate

bachche bhi gharibi ko samajhne lage shayad  
ab jaag bhi jaate hain to sahari nahin khate

har ek ke paison ki dava bhi nahin khate

ham logon ke bachche kabhi sardi nahin khate
